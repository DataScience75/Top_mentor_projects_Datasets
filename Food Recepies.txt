1. Butter Toast
Toast bread, spread butter, serve warm.

2. Garlic Toast
Mix butter and garlic, spread on bread, toast until golden.

3. Scrambled Eggs
Beat eggs, cook in butter, stir until soft.

4. Boiled Eggs
Boil eggs in water for 8–10 minutes, cool and peel.

5. Fried Egg
Heat oil, crack egg, cook until white sets.

6. Omelette
Beat eggs, pour into pan, cook and fold.

7. Cheese Sandwich
Place cheese between bread, toast or grill.

8. Veg Sandwich
Add sliced veggies to bread, spread mayo, serve.

9. Peanut Butter Sandwich
Spread peanut butter on bread, close and eat.

10. Fruit Salad
Chop fruits, mix, chill and serve.

11. Banana Milkshake
Blend banana, milk, and sugar.

12. Mango Milkshake
Blend mango pulp, milk, and sugar.

13. Lemonade
Mix lemon juice, water, sugar, and ice.

14. Tea
Boil water, add tea leaves, milk, sugar, strain.

15. Coffee
Mix coffee powder, hot water, milk, sugar.

16. Instant Noodles
Boil noodles with seasoning, cook 2–3 minutes.

17. Vegetable Soup
Boil chopped veggies with salt and pepper.

18. Tomato Soup
Boil tomatoes, blend, heat with salt.

19. Rice
Boil rice in water, drain when soft.

20. Fried Rice
Stir-fry cooked rice with veggies and soy sauce.

21. Plain Pasta
Boil pasta, drain, add butter and salt.

22. Masala Pasta
Cook pasta, mix with tomato sauce and spices.

23. Grilled Cheese
Butter bread, add cheese, grill until melted.

24. Popcorn
Heat kernels in oil until popped, add salt.

25. French Fries
Fry sliced potatoes until golden, add salt.

26. Mashed Potatoes
Boil potatoes, mash with butter and salt.

27. Pancakes
Mix flour, milk, sugar, cook on pan.

28. Waffles
Pour batter in waffle maker, cook until crisp.

29. Chocolate Milk
Mix cocoa powder, sugar, and milk.

30. Hot Chocolate
Heat milk with cocoa powder and sugar.

31. Cereal Bowl
Add milk to cereal, serve.

32. Yogurt Bowl
Mix yogurt with fruits or honey.

33. Veg Stir Fry
Stir-fry veggies in oil with salt.

34. Fried Bananas
Slice bananas, fry lightly in butter.

35. Apple Slices Snack
Slice apples, sprinkle sugar or cinnamon.

36. Cheese Omelette
Cook omelette, add cheese, fold.

37. Bread Upma
Toast bread pieces, mix with spices and veggies.

38. Poha
Rinse poha, cook with onion and spices.

39. Corn Salad
Mix boiled corn with salt and lemon.

40. Corn Butter
Mix boiled corn with butter and salt.

41. Egg Salad
Chop boiled eggs, mix with mayo and salt.

42. Cucumber Salad
Slice cucumber, add salt and lemon.

43. Curd Rice
Mix cooked rice with yogurt and salt.

44. Rice and Milk
Mix warm rice with milk and sugar.

45. Sweet Toast
Toast bread, add sugar or honey.

46. Honey Lemon Water
Mix warm water, honey, lemon juice.

47. Simple Pizza Toast
Add sauce and cheese on bread, bake.

48. Veg Wrap
Fill flatbread with veggies and sauce.

49. Chocolate Toast
Spread chocolate spread on toast.

50. Ice Cream Bowl
Scoop ice cream, add toppings if desired.
